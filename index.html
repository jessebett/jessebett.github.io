Biography

I am a graduate student in Machine Learning at the University of Toronto and the Vector Institute. I am currently pursuing follow-up research to my work on Neural Ordinary Differential Equations, and am generally interested in approximate inference for latent variable models. My supervisors are Drs. David Duvenaud and Roger Grosse. I am the instructor for CSC412/2506: Probabilistic Learning and Reasoning.
